Privacy First

Your inbox is nobody's business

Pebble stores everything locally on your machine. No cloud servers, no telemetry, no third-party access.

AES-256 Encrypted Tokens

OAuth tokens and credentials encrypted at rest with a per-device key.

100% Local Data

SQLite database, search index, and attachments all stored on your device.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Powerful features designed around how you actually work with email.

Kanban Board

Drag emails across Todo, Waiting, and Done columns. Turn your inbox into a task manager.

Smart Search

Full-text search powered by Tantivy. Find any email instantly by content, sender, or date.

Multi-Provider

Gmail, Outlook, and any IMAP server. Manage all your accounts from a single interface.

Rules Engine

Create automation rules to auto-label, move, or flag incoming emails by sender, subject, and more.

Snooze & Star

Snooze emails to resurface later. Star important ones. Stay on top of what matters.

Translation

Built-in bilingual view with DeepL/LLM-powered translation. Read emails in any language.

i18n Ready

Full interface in English and Chinese, with easy extensibility for more languages.

Keyboard First

Navigate, compose, search, and manage entirely from the keyboard. Vim-inspired shortcuts.

Dark & Light

Gorgeous light and dark themes with system-aware auto-switching. Easy on the eyes, always.
